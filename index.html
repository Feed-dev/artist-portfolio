about

Welcome to the portfolio of James Neural, an AI generative artist who enjoys experimenting with technology and art. This section contains a collection of my personal projects created with AI and generative algorithms. Each piece reflects my fascination and experimentation with technology and art. I hope my artworks inspire you to reconsider the role of technology in art and creativity. My goal is to make art that is visually appealing, thought-provoking, and challenges preconceived notions about art. Thank you for taking the time to look at my portfolio.

In addition to being an artist, I am a blockchain developer. I'm intrigued by the future possibilities of using AI to create fantastical worlds and integrating Blockchain systems into them. That is why I am working to create smart contracts and prompting capabilities. I maintain a personal blog where I share my thoughts, ideas, and research on these technologies and their societal impact. I believe that combining AI, blockchain, and the metaverse will result in a more transparent, secure, and efficient society. I'm constantly learning and exploring new ways to use these technologies to create vast, infinite worlds. My artworks not only demonstrate my artistic abilities, but also my interest in these cutting-edge technologies and their potential societal impact.
